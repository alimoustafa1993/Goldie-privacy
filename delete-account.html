Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your Goldie Steps account and all associated data, please send an email to:

support@goldiesteps.app

Please send the request from the same Google account used to sign in.

What data will be deleted

  • User profile information
  • Authentication identifiers
  • Application usage data

Data retention

Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes for up to 30 days, after which it will be permanently deleted.
