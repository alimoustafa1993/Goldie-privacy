Privacy Policy

Goldie (also known as Ancient Gold Coin) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Goldie mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Goldie collects only the minimum information required to operate core application features.

  • Account Information: Users sign in using Google Authentication. We receive a unique user ID and basic profile information required for account identification.
  • Wallet & Transactions: The app stores virtual wallet balances and transaction history related to in-app usage of virtual currencies (AGC and SGC).
  • Location Data (Optional): Location access may be requested only to provide the Qibla direction feature. Location data is not stored or shared.
  • Notifications: The app may send push notifications related to account activity, subscriptions, or important updates.

Goldie does not collect contacts, SMS data, call logs, photos, audio, or device files.

2. In-App Virtual Currencies

Goldie includes two virtual in-app currencies used exclusively inside the application:

  • AGC (Ancient Gold Coin): A virtual in-app currency purchased through Google Play Billing or earned through in-app features such as referrals. AGC is used only to unlock digital content, subscriptions, and features inside the app. AGC has no real-world monetary value and cannot be withdrawn as real money.
  • SGC (Silver Gold Coin): A virtual reward currency earned through app participation such as referrals. SGC can be converted into AGC for in-app use only. SGC is virtual and has no real-world monetary value.

3. Payments

All purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing. Goldie does not store, process, or have access to users’ credit card or payment information.

4. Subscriptions

Goldie offers optional digital subscriptions that unlock premium features within the app. Subscriptions are virtual, non-transferable, and managed securely through Google Play. Subscription status is validated by the server and used only to control access to app features.

5. Children’s Privacy

Goldie may include educational and religious content suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Third-Party Services

Goldie uses trusted third-party services to operate properly:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Authentication

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Access to sensitive operations is restricted and monitored.

8. Data Sharing

Goldie does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties except where required to operate the app, provide core services, or comply with legal obligations.

9. User Rights

Users may request account deletion or data review by contacting us. Some wallet or transaction data may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
