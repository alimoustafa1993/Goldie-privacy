Privacy Policy

Goldie (also known as Ancient Gold Coin) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Goldie mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Goldie collects only the minimum information required to operate core app features.

  • Account Information: Users sign in using Google Authentication. We receive basic profile information such as a unique user ID and email address for account identification.
  • Wallet & Transactions: The app stores wallet balances, transaction history, and transfers between users for in-app currency management.
  • Location Data (Optional): Location access may be requested only to provide the Qibla direction feature. Location data is not stored or shared.
  • Notifications: The app may send push notifications related to account activity or app updates.

Goldie does not collect contacts, SMS data, call logs, photos, or device files.

2. In-App Currencies

Goldie includes two in-app currencies:

  • AGC (Primary Currency): Purchased through Google Play Billing and used only inside the app. AGC can be transferred between users but cannot be withdrawn as real money.
  • SGC (Reward Currency): Earned through app participation such as referrals. SGC may be converted or withdrawn manually by the app administration according to the app’s rules.

3. Payments

All purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing. Goldie does not store or process credit card or payment information.

4. Children’s Privacy

Goldie may include educational and religious content. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

5. Third-Party Services

Goldie uses trusted third-party services to operate properly:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Authentication

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Access to sensitive operations is restricted and monitored.

7. Data Sharing

Goldie does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties except where required to operate the app or comply with legal obligations.

8. User Rights

Users may request account deletion or data review by contacting us. Wallet and transaction data may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
